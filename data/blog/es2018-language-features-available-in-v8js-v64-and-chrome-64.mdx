***[ES2018 Language Features](https://262.ecma-international.org/9.0/)***

I just ***found out*** minutes ago on **Twitter** that

>[Every single one of the new ES2018 language features is available by default in v8](https://t.co/Y0qdVSQHE7) [@v8js](https://twitter.com/v8js?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw) v6.4 and Chrome 64. — [V8 (@v8js)](https://twitter.com/v8js/status/956976954074267648?ref_src=twsrc%5Etfw) January 26, 2018

*Happy `ES2018`, `v8js`, and `Chrome 64ing`*!
