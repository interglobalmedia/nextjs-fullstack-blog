Yesterday, I shared my first link post, AND I shared my first image post on
Tumblr. I had just generated an Artifical Intelligence (AI) image using a
service called [Night Cafe Creator](https://creator.nightcafe.studio/), and they
generate images in webp format by default. Twitter accepts images in webp
format, but Tumblr does not. Neither does Linkedin, _BTW_. But I remembered that
I could simply **_transform_** my `webp` **_image_** into a `jpeg` (`jpg`)
**_image_** using my `macOS Finder`. To **_learn_** how to **_do this_**, please
**_visit_** my `article` **_entitled_**
[Converting image formats on macOS using the Finder app](https://www.interglobalmedianetwork.com/blog/converting-image-formats-on-macos-using-the-finder-app)
on my business site
[interglobalmedianetwork.com](https://www.interglobalmedianetwork.com).

So when you **_share_** an `image` on `Tumblr`, **_make sure_** that you have a
`copy` of it in `jpeg` (`jpg`) **_format_**, and **_keep_** it **_handy_** for
those `Linkedin` **_posts_** that you may **_want_** to **_create_** with it.
`Twitter`, on the other hand, **_accepts_** the `webp` **_format_**.

_Happy tumblring!_
